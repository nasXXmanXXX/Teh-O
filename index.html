Our Story

Started with a cast-iron pot and no plan.

Ember & Grain grew out of a single obsession: why does supermarket bread taste like nothing? After years of weekend baking and a sourdough starter named Margaret, founders Priya and Tom left their office jobs to open a 400 sq ft bakehouse on Maple Lane.

Every loaf is hand-shaped, cold-proofed overnight, and baked in a deck oven rescued from a shuttered Italian trattoria. We source our wheat from a single family farm 40 km away and mill it fresh each week.
